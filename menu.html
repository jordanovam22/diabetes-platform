How much can I eat if I have diabetes?

Eating the right amount of food will help you manage your blood glucose & your weight. Your health care team can help you figure out how much food & calories you should eat each day.

What foods and drinks should I limit if I have diabetes?

Foods and drinks to limit include fried foods,foods high in salt, also called sodium, sweets, such as baked goods, candy, and ice cream, energy drinks etc.
