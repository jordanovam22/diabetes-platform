What Is Glucose?

Glucose comes from the Greek word for "sweet." It's a type of sugar you get from foods you eat, and your body uses it for energy. As it travels through your bloodstream to your cells, it's called blood sugar.

Is there a pill for diabetes?

Injected insulin is the main treatment for type 1 diabetes. There is no other therapy for most type 1 diabetes patients. Many people with type 2 diabetes can take pills for therapy.

Can insulin cure diabetes?

Insulin doesn't' cure diabetes. People with type 1 diabetes have to inject insulin. Before the discovery of insulin, these people all died young from a condition called diabetic ketoacidosis.
